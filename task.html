Chocolate chip cookies

Prep Time : 15 minutes

Cook Time : 10 minutes

Steps
  1. In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup butter, 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup brown sugar, 1 egg, and 1 tsp vanilla
  2. Add 1.5 cups flour, 1/2 tsp baking soda, pinch of salt. Mix well.
  3. Stir in 1 cup chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop onto a tray and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 10–12 minutes.

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup (softened)
All-purpose flour 1.5 cups
Chocolate chips 1 cup
